Milk Tea

Description

Tea drinkers can liven up tea time by adding milk to their tea after brewing. Adding milk is a great way to play with new flavours.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup water
  • 1 Black tea teabag from a brand of your choice
  • 1/4 cup milk of your choice
  • 3 tsp honey or simple syrup

Steps

  1. Bring water to a boil and pour into cup with teabag in it.
  2. Steep the teabag in the hot water for 4-6 minutes. The longer the tea is left to steep, the stronger the tea will be.
  3. Remove teabag.
  4. Stir in milk and honey/simple sugar.
