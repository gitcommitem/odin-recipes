Thai Tea

Description

Thai tea is incredibly popular and easy to make at home. Many store-bought tea mixes are dyed with yellow food colouring to help give the drink its distinct orange colour.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup Thai tea mix
  • 4 cups water
  • 3/4 cup of granulated sugar
  • 3/4 cup of half and half (can be substituted with coconut milk, whole milk, or sweetened condensed milk)
  • Ice

Steps

  1. Bring water to a boil on a stovetop and add in tea mix and sugar. Stir until sugar is dissolved and let simmer for 3 minutes.
  2. Turn off stovetop and let the tea steep for at least 30 minutes. Generally the more concentrated it is the better it tastes.
  3. Strain the tea leave mix from the tea and let the tea cool.
  4. Fill glasses with desired amount of ice and pour in the cooled Thai tea, leaving enough room for your creamer of choice.
  5. Fill the remaining portion of the glass with your creamer of choice. For an 8 oz. glass, 2-3 tbsp of creamer is recommended.
